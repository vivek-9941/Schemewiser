PM-YASASVI: Top Class School Education for OBC, EBC and DNT Students

Details:

The PM-YASASVI scheme provides educational assistance to students belonging to Other Backward Classes (OBCs), Economically Backward Classes (EBCs), and Denotified, Nomadic Tribes (DNT). It is funded by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment on a 100% basis.

Objective:

The objective is to provide premium education to meritorious students belonging to OBC, EBC, and DNT categories from Class 9 onwards till they complete Class 12.

Scope and Coverage:

All OBC, EBC, and DNT students studying in Top Class Schools with a household annual income of less than â‚¹2.5 lakhs are eligible. Scholarship allocation is based on merit using the online portal.

Application Process:

Online application submission through the National Scholarship Portal. Detailed steps are available on the National Scholarship Portal.

Documents Required:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport size photograph
  • Educational qualification marksheets/certificates
  • Valid Income certificate
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Caste/Community Certificate
  • Disability certificate, if applicable
  • Bank Account details
  • Any other required documents
