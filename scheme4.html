Chief Minister's Overseas Scholarship Scheme for Minorities

Scheme Summary:

Details:

  • Chief Minister's Overseas Scholarship Scheme for Minor
  • Launched by Minorities Welfare Department, Government of Telangana in 2015-16.
  • Aim: Support students for postgraduate/doctoral studies in foreign universities.

Benefits:

  • Financial assistance for fees, living expenses, educational loan eligibility, Research/Teachingship, and one-way fare.

Eligibility:

  • Resident of Telangana, community member.
  • Age limit: 35 years, one child family, income < â‚¹5 lakhs p.a.
  • Specific qualification criteria, admission at accredited foreign universities, valid Passport, TOEFL/IEL & GRE/G requirements.
  • Eligible countries: USA, UK, Australia, Canada, Singapore, Germany, Zealand, Japan, France, South Korea.

Application Process:

  • Online application via Telangana ePass website during specified periods with comprehensive document submission.

Documents Required:

  • Caste, income, birth certificates, Aadhaar card, E-pass ID, residence certificate, passport copy, academic records, qualifying exam score, admission offer letter, tax assessment, bank pass book, and photograph.
