NAMO E-Tablet Scheme

Details:

  • Tablets provided to college and university students in Gujarat.
  • Free tablets preloaded with educational content.

Benefits:

  • Tablets provided to for free in Gujarat.

Eligibility:

  • Students must have passed 12th examination from a recognized institution in Gujarat.
  • First-year college polytechnic students eligible.

Application Process:

  • Online application through respective colleges in Gujarat.
  • Details of eligible candidates by the institute.
  • Required fee of Rs. 1000 to be deposited by the institute.

Documents Required:

  • Domicile certificate, address proof, voter ID, Aadhar card, 12th certificate, admission confirmation, below the poverty line certificate, and caste certificate.
